The dull stupidity of the Negroe leaves him without anu desire for instruction.

Whether the Creator originally formed these black people a little lower than other men, or that they have lost their intellectual powers through disuse, I will not assume the provice of determining; but certain it is, that a NEW NEGRE, (as those lately imported from Africa are called,) is a complete definition of insolent stupidity, nor could a more forcible means be employed for the conversion of of a deift, than fetting ne of these creatures before him, as an example of man in a state of nature, unbiassed by revelation or education

Their stupidity does not however authorize us to consider them as beasts for our use, much less to deny them all knowledge of the common salvation.
